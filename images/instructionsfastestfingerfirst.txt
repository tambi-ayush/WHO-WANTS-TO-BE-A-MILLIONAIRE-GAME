 This game is inspired from the English show who wants to be a millionaire.On the first window you can
  check our profile by clicking on AboutUs button.
  
 In Fastest Finger First window, there is one question and four options corresponding to that question.
 You have to arrange the options in their correct order.
 
 There will be a timer of 30 seconds to get correct order of the options.If you failed to do so you will
 be directed back to the initial window.
 
 If you get correct order of the options in  given time of 30 seconds you will be moved to the "who wants
 to be a millionaire Quiz window".Where you have to select your subject of interest.
 You can click each option at most once.
 
 Enjoy the Game and give your views on mail in about us section.
